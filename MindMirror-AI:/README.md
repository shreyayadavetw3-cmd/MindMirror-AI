# 🧠 MindMirror AI

### Reflect. Understand. Thrive.

MindMirror AI is a Generative AI-powered mental wellness companion designed for students preparing for high-stakes examinations such as NEET, JEE, UPSC, CAT, CUET, and GATE.

The platform helps students understand their emotional well-being through conversational journaling, identifies hidden stress triggers, detects burnout risks, and provides personalized wellness support throughout their academic journey.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Students preparing for competitive examinations often experience:

* Academic pressure
* Burnout
* Anxiety and self-doubt
* Sleep deprivation
* Fear of failure
* Lack of emotional support

Traditional mood trackers only collect ratings and fail to uncover deeper emotional patterns.

MindMirror AI leverages Generative AI to transform daily reflections into meaningful mental wellness insights.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

MindMirror AI acts as an always-available digital wellness companion.

Students can:

* Record daily thoughts through journaling
* Track emotions and stress levels
* Identify recurring stress triggers
* Receive personalized coping strategies
* Access mindfulness recommendations
* Monitor long-term emotional trends

---

## ✨ Key Features

### Daily AI Journal Analysis

Analyze open-ended journal entries to identify:

* Primary emotions
* Stress levels
* Confidence levels
* Burnout risk
* Hidden stress triggers

### Personalized Wellness Support

Generate:

* Context-aware coping strategies
* Personalized encouragement
* Mindfulness exercises
* Study-life balance recommendations

### Stress Pattern Detection

Identify recurring triggers such as:

* Mock test anxiety
* Parental expectations
* Time management challenges
* Sleep deprivation

### Weekly Wellness Report

Provide:

* Emotional trends
* Stress insights
* Burnout indicators
* Positive habit recognition
* Actionable recommendations

### AI Wellness Companion

A conversational AI that offers empathetic support and guidance throughout exam preparation.
